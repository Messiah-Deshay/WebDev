Welcome to EHAC

Welcome to the European Historical Anomaly Committee (EHAC) - an independent research initiative dedicated to investigating unexplained patterns and phenomena hidden within the fabric of history. Since the 1960s, EHAC has quietly gathered evidence connecting iconic events, artifacts, and figures to mysterious shifts in global reality. Our most compelling focus: the Mona Lisa and the controversial Shift Theory, which suggests that each movement of the painting may alter the course of human events. At EHAC, we seek truth in the shadows of the past, piecing together the anomalies that others overlook.

We are dedicated to uncovering the mysteries of historical anomalies, focusing on the most significant and unexplained phenomena throughout history.
