In Memoriam: Dr. Adrien Vallon

EHAC Chief Investigator, 1997 to 2021

Dr. Adrien Vallon led EHAC's most significant operations during the Fourth and Fifth Shifts, including the retrieval of the Lost Codex and the containment of the Madrid Echo. He disappeared shortly after the Louvre's lockdown in late 2021, an event believed to be linked to the Mona Lisa's temporal resonance field.

Though officially listed as missing, many within EHAC believe Dr. Vallon became unstuck in time, a casualty of the very anomalies he swore to protect the world from. His notes remain essential to modern Shift Theory research.

Through remembrance, we preserve his legacy and continue his pursuit of knowledge and truth.
