Research & Discoveries

EHAC's research explores the world's most compelling historical anomalies, from the unexplained journeys of iconic artifacts to the patterns that shape our collective past. Explore our featured investigations and ongoing projects below.

Featured Investigations

The Mona Lisa Enigma

Tracing the mysterious relocations of the Mona Lisa and their impact on European history. Our team analyzes archival records, eyewitness accounts, and scientific data to uncover the truth behind the painting's influence on major historical shifts.

The Shift Effect

Investigating the recurring phenomenon where the movement of culturally significant artifacts coincides with global events. Is there a hidden force connecting art, memory, and the course of history?

Lost Relics & Forgotten Archives

From vanished manuscripts to misplaced masterpieces, EHAC researchers pursue leads on lost artifacts whose stories may rewrite our understanding of the past.

Ongoing Projects

  • Cataloging the movements of key European artworks (1500 to 1950)
  • Building a digital archive of eyewitness testimonies and rare documents
  • Collaborating with international museums on artifact provenance
  • Developing interactive timelines of historical anomalies
